## 📌 About The Project

**Ezy-Parking** is a complete, smart parking management system designed to alleviate the growing problem of parking space scarcity in metropolitan and tier-1 cities. It provides smart solutions for the short-time rental of empty spaces (commercial parking lots, apartments, office spaces, etc.) to help users find safe, affordable, and accessible parking while on the go.

By eliminating the need for aimless driving to find a parking spot, Ezy-Parking reduces traffic congestion, prevents unauthorized roadside parking, and ensures a smoother daily commute.

## ✔️ Key Features

- **Smart Discovery & Suggestions:** Search for parking based on your schedule, area, or pincode. The map renders real-time pricing and availability alongside nearby events.
- **Dynamic Slot Tracking:** Track empty parking spots in real-time. Slots are dynamically released and updated as vehicles enter and exit.
- **Advance Booking:** Reserve parking slots prior to reaching the authorized lot to ensure guaranteed availability.
- **Automated Gate Access:** Seamless entry and exit utilizing sensing techniques to automatically open gates/handles for authorized vehicles.
- **Security & Analytics:** 
  - Cross-checks complete vehicle history upon exit using registration numbers (owner authority, licenses, pending challans).
  - Alerts users via email if their vehicle is parked in a "No Parking Zone" or towed by the RTO.
- **Find My Vehicle:** Easily track your parked vehicle's location inside a massive parking lot using its registration number and color.
- **Community Sharing:** Users can share the best hidden spots via community posts.

## 🔑 Tech Stack

- **Core Technologies:** Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Frameworks & Tools:** Django, Bootstrap, IoT integration
- **Advanced Tech:** Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision
